Shakuni's 3 tips for BEM
September 19, 2020

I think it’s fair to say that even though BEM won’t solve all our problems it is extraordinarily useful for constructing scalable and maintainable interfaces where everyone on the team should have a clear idea of how things can be improved. This is because a great deal of front end development is not just about the nice tricks that solve one little problem in the short term; we need agreements, promises and binding social contracts between developers so that our codebase can adapt over time. Generally I like to think of BEM as an answer to Nicolas Gallagher’s question: Replace "can you build this?" with "can you maintain this without losing your minds?"
